
## Farm Merge Valley simulator

In farm merge valley, your goal is to collect 108 stickers within 51 days.
You get stickers from two sources: consecutive logins and daily challenges.
This is a list of all hard values needed to get an overview of sticker rarity and collection probability over 51 days.


**Stickers and Sticker Packs**

Name definitions:
Sticker: the collectable item
Daily challenge and daily login sticker packs: pack that contains stickers
Sticker pack bought with stars: guarantees a non-duplicate sticker of the sticker-pack value and additional stickers
Daily challenges: Challenges starting every 24 hours
Season: the 51 day duration you have to collect 108 stickers

Consecutive logins:
Day 1: one-star sticker pack
Day 2: two-star sticker pack
Day 3: three-star sticker pack
Day 4: four-star sticker pack
Day 5: five-star sticker pack
(repeat)

Daily challenges:
Every day you get 10 sticker packs from daily challenges.
It's impossible to get a five-star sticker pack from daily challenges.
The 10 sticker packs are random between one, two, three and four-star. (25%)

A daily challenge sticker pack contains the following:
1 star sticker pack: contains 2 one-star stickers
2 star sticker pack: contains 2 one-star and 1 two-star sticker.
3 star sticker pack: contains: 
4 star sticker pack: contains:

The 108 stickers are:
one-star: 27
two-star: 21 
three-star: 18
four-star: 18
five-star: 25

**Star currency**

Duplicate system:
If you get a sticker you already own, you get a star instead depending on the sticker's star rating. A one-star sticker gives 1 star, a two-star sticker gives 2 stars and so on.

You can buy three, four and five sticker packs with these stars, here is what these packs contain:
three-star: guarantees a three-star non-duplicate sticker
four-star: guarantees a four-star non-duplicate sticker
five-star: guarantees a five-star non-duplicate sticker
